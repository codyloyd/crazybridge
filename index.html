Crazy Bridge!


Each hand consists of 13 tricks.
Each trick is worth 10 points.
Each player keeps their own score.
Switch partners after each hand.

hand 1

  1. Spades are trumps.
  2. No trumps. Winners add 20pts to score.
  3. Clubs are trump.
  4. Hearts are trump. Do NOT look at cards.

hand 2

  1. Spades are trump. Losers subtract 10 pts from score.
  2. Clubs are trump. Winners add 100 pts.
  3. No trump. Winners take all score.
  4. Diamonds are trump. Winners and losers trade score.

hand 3

  1. Clubs are trump. Do NOT look at cards.
  2. Hearts are trump. Winner doubles score.
  3. Cut for trump. Winners add 60pts.
  4. No trump. Losers take all score!

hand 4

  1. Spades are trump. Losers subtract 10 from score.
  2. Hearts are trump. Winners and Losers trade scores.
  3. Diamonds are trump. Winners add 100 to score.
  4. Clubs are trump. Winners double score.

hand 5

  1. Diamonds are trump. Winner takes all.
  2. No trumps. Winners add 200 to score.
  3. Clubs are trump. Losers take all.
  4. Hearts are trump. Winners and losers trade scores.
