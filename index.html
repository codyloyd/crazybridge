Crazy Bridge!


Each hand consists of 13 tricks.
Each trick is worth 10 points.
Each player keeps their own score.
Switch partners after each round.

round 1

  1. ♠️ are trump.
  2. No trump. Winners add 20 to score.
  3. ♣️ are trump.
  4. ♥️ are trump. Do NOT look at cards.

round 2

  1. ♠️ are trump. Losers subtract 10 from score.
  2. ♣️ are trump. Winners add 100.
  3. No trump. Winners take all score.
  4. ♦️ are trump. Winners and losers trade score.

round 3

  1. ♣️ are trump. Do NOT look at cards.
  2. ♥️ are trump. Winner doubles score.
  3. Cut for trump. Winners add 60.
  4. No trump. Losers take all score!

round 4

  1. ♠️ are trump. Losers subtract 10 from score.
  2. ♥️ are trump. Winners and Losers trade scores.
  3. ♦️ are trump. Winners add 100 to score.
  4. ♣️ are trump. Winners double score.

round 5

  1. ♦️ are trump. Winner takes all.
  2. No trumps. Winners add 200 to score.
  3. ♣️ are trump. Losers take all.
  4. ♥️ are trump. Winners and losers trade scores.
